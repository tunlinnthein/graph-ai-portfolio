About Me

Bridging Clinical Research & Artificial Intelligence

I am a Senior Research Fellow at the Infectious Disease Research and Training Office (IDRTO), National Centre for Infectious Diseases (NCID) in Singapore. My work sits at the intersection of epidemiology, clinical trials, and public health.

With over 12 years of experience spanning from epidemiological surveys to clinical trials, I specialize in dengue and emerging infectious diseases. I oversee research databases, ensure data integrity, and manage complex quantitative and qualitative studies.

Currently, I am expanding my toolkit to include Generative AI to revolutionize how we handle research data, automate literature reviews, and enhance scientific communication.
